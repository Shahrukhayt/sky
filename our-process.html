Project

At the initial stage, we receive your content and analyze it thoroughly.

Quotation

The dedicated Account Manager comes up with a reasonable quotation based on the scope and budget specified by your company.

Approval

We wait for your approval to proceed. Once our competitive quotation meets your budget requirements we wait for your approval.

Project Submission

After final approval, we submit the project to a specially assigned team. The team studies the project and determine technicalities and comes up with a deadline, our experts always complete the project well before the deadline.

Translation

Our certified linguistic experts and native speakers translate your content with clarity and zero ambiguity.

Editing

Our Experts edit the translated content for clarity if required. Add Glossary and Appendix as per requirements.

Proofreading

Our proofreaders check every phrase for no error or ambiguity to be left behind.

Quality Assurance

The final step is to ensure the quality that meets our standard. Our Quality Assurance team ensures the best quality before delivering the content.

Project Delivery

Your dedicated supervisor delivers the content and looks for feedback.
