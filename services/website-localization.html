Website Localization

It's easy to make your website accessible to various markets, but if the entirety of your website isn't translated properly, your content isn't relevant to your visitors. If your content isn't relevant, then visitors are likely to leave your website and forget all about your company and what you have to offer.

The solution is complete website localization services. At Lentrax, we're a full-service translation service provider, but we do much more than that. We strive to improve the customer experience on your website by providing you with valuable, locally applicable content, images, and more. From strategic planning to launching, SEO, and thorough translations, you can count on us to help you bring your website into new and existing markets.

Multilingual Customer Support

Did you know that 72 percent of internet users are not native English speakers? Not only can we provide you with translation services, we offer multilingual chat and customer support options in 110 languages. With our help, you can route queries, direct clients to other departments, boost productivity, and create a better overall customer experience.

Images, Layout, and Desktop Publishing

Whether you need Chinese language translation, French language translation, or something else, your website is much more than the content itself. At Lentrax, we focus on the big picture. We go the extra mile to make sure your images, interactive charts, and graphic elements are relevant to your target audience. From design to format, printing, and more, we can provide you with complete desktop publishing services for nearly any market in the world.

Mobile Site Localization

Mobile display screens can greatly restrict the content on your website, but with almost seven billion mobile users worldwide, it's important to optimize your content for this platform. Different languages look different on a screen, which is why it's so important to optimize your site for every demographic you're targeting. Our translators take into account the lettering, fonts, and text flow within a limited-space setting, ensuring that your mobile users experience the best version of your website as possible, no matter what kind of mobile device they're using.

CMS, E-Commerce, and System Integration

Every aspect of your website will be translated to perfection when you partner with Lentrax. Our service of translation include:

  • Full-service translations
  • Merging internal linguistic assets
  • Tailoring your workflows
  • Incorporating back-end structures
  • Computerizing manual routes and linking

Multilingual SEO and SEM

Multilingual SEO is an essential component of the website localization process. As a global brand catering to different international markets, you need to make sure that your keyword research is provided by a local speaker of the local language.

Our global language translation provider starts by understanding your search goals. Then, we utilize in-country inhabitant speakers with SEO knowledge to research appropriate keyword usage. By directing our research in this way, we're able to find the best keywords to help you achieve a higher search ranking and increased click rates, which increases page views and your bottom line.
